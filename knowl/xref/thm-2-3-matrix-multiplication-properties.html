Theorem 1.4.3. Properties of matrix multiplication.

Assume that \(A,B,C\) are matrices of appropriate sizes and \(r\) is a scalar. Then
  1. \(A(BC)=(AB)C\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \((A+B)C=AC+BC\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  3. \(C(A+B)=CA+CB\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  4. \(A(rB)=r(AB)=(rA)B\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  5. \((AB)^T=B^TA^T\text{.}\)
