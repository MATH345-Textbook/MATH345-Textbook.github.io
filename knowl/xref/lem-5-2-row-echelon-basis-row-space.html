Lemma 2.4.24.

If \(R\) is a matrix in row echelon form (recall Definition 2.2.19) then the non-zero rows of \(R\) are a basis of \(\row(R)\text{.}\)
🔗
🔗

Why is this true?.

The nonzero rows of \(R\) are linearly independent by Fact 2.4.4, and span \(\row(R)\) by definition. They thus by definition form a basis for \(\row(R)\text{.}\)
