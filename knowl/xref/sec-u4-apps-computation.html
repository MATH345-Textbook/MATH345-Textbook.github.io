xhat = np.linalg.lstsq(A, b, rcond=None)[0]
r = b - A @ xhat
A.T @ r
The vector \(\mathbf{r}\) is the residual. The output of A.T @ r should be close to zero because the least-squares residual is orthogonal to the columns of \(A\text{.}\)
