Fact 6.1.7.

Any non-zero linear combination of eigenvectors associated with the same eigenvalue \(\lambda\) is also an eigenvector with eigenvalue \(\lambda\text{.}\) Conversely, all eigenvectors associated with the eigenvalue \(\lambda\) can be written uniquely as a linear combinations of the basic eigenvectors associated with the eigenvalue \(\lambda\text{.}\)
