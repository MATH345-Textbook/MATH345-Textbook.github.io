Theorem 5.4.2.

For an inner product \(\langle \; , \; \rangle\) on a vector space \(V\text{,}\) the following additional properties automatically hold:
  1. \(\langle\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}+\mathbf{w}\rangle=\langle\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}\rangle+\langle\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{w}\rangle\) for any \(\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}\) in \(V\text{.}\)
  2. \(\langle\mathbf{u}, r\mathbf{v}\rangle=r\langle\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}\rangle\) for any \(\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}\) in \(V\text{,}\) and \(r\) in \(\mathbb{R}\text{.}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \langle \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{0}\rangle =0=\langle \mathbf{0},\mathbf{v}\rangle\)
  4. \(\langle \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{v}\rangle=0\) if and only if \(\mathbf{v}=\mathbf{0}\text{.}\)
