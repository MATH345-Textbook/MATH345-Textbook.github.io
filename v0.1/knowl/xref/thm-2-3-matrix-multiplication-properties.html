Theorem 2.3.5. Properties of Matrix Multiplication.

Assume that \(A,B,C\) are matrices of appropriate sizes. Then:
  1. \(A(BC)=(AB)C\text{,}\) i.e. matrix multiplication is associative
  2. \(\displaystyle (A+B)C=AC + BC\)
  3. \(\displaystyle C(A+B)=CA + CB\)
  4. \(\displaystyle A(rB)=r(AB)=(rA)B\)
  5. \(\displaystyle (AB)^T=B^TA^T\)
