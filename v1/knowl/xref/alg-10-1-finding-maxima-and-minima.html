Algorithm 9.4.2.

Given a function \(f\text{,}\) continuous on a closed bounded set \(D\text{,}\) this algorithm will find the absolute maxima and minima of the function \(f\text{.}\)
  1. Find all critical points of \(f\) on the interior of the set \(D\text{.}\)
  2. Find the absolute maximum of \(f\) among all boundary points of the set \(D\text{.}\)
  3. The largest and smallest values of \(f\) given by the points found in the first two steps give the absolute minimum and maximum values of the function, and the inputs that gives these values are the absolute minima and maxima.
