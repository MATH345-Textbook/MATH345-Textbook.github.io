Lemma 4.3.4.

If \(R\) is a matrix in row echelon form (recall Definition 1.4.1) then the non-zero rows of \(R\) are a basis of \(\row(R)\text{.}\)

Proof.

The nonzero rows of \(R\) are linearly independent by Fact 4.2.4, and span \(\row(R)\) by definition. They thus by definition form a basis for \(\row(R)\text{.}\)
