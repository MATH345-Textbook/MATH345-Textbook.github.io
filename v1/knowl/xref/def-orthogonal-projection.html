Definition 5.2.5. The Orthogonal Projection Onto a Subspace.

Let \(U\) be a subspace of \(\R^n\text{.}\) Then the orthogonal projection of the vector \(\mathbf{x} \in \R^n\) onto \(U\) is the vector \(\proj_U \mathbf{x} \in U\) which is closest to \(\mathbf{x}\text{,}\) in the sense that for any \(\mathbf{z} \in U\) with \(\mathbf{z} \neq \mathbf{y}\text{,}\) \(\| \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y} \| \lt \| \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{z} \|\text{.}\)
